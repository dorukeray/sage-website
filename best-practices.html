Best Practices

Tips & Tricks about Sage.

Read about how to implement commonly features and patterns in your Sage server. Sage is quite new, so best practices are still developing.

Sage's specification is intentionally agnostic on a handful of important topics facing APIs such as dealing with the network, authorization, and pagination; because they're outside of the description about what Sage is and instead just common practice.

The recommendations on this page should not be taken as "sacred words", and in some cases may rightfully be ignored in favor of some other approach.

Some sections introduce some of the philosophy we are developing within Dorkodu around designing and deploying services with Sage, while others are more tactical suggestions for solving common problems.

Work In Progress

Sage is new and the work is still in progress. We are working on implementations right now and will test our approach in real world before publishing some ideas and assumptions as "best practices".
